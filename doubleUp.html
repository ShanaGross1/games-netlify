D O U B L E U P

---------

Game Rules

How To Play:

Use the arrows on the keyboard, or the buttons on the page to combine like numbers to make doubles. With every click, an additional number is added to the board. Keep going until you reach 1024! The game is over when the board is full and there are no adjacent numbers with the same value. Good Luck!!
